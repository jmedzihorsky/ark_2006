ark_2006
==========

This dataset contains data published by the 
[Social Policy Analysis Centre (S.P.A.C.E.)](http://www.nspace.sk/menu.html)
as an update of the first edition of the 
_Atlas of Roma communities in Slovakia_.
It contains data on 207 segregated settlements 
out of the 281 covered by the first edition
of the _Atlas_.  Data for most of the settlements
is available for 2004 and 2005. 


[Codebook](/documentation/ark_2006_codebook.pdf)

Data in a tab-separated format is [here](/data/ark_2006.dat)


Please cite the dataset as

Medzihorsky, Juraj (2013) _Atlas of Romani Communities in Slovakia 2006 Update Dataset_ 

The original data:

Social Policy Analysis Center (SPACE). (2006). _Aktualizacia Atlasu romskych komunít na Slovensku_
(Update of the Atlas of Roma communities in Slovakia). Bratislava: SPACE.

The first edition of the Atlas:

Radicova, Iveta. (2004). _Atlas romskych komunit na Slovensku 2004_ 
(Atlas of Roma communities in Slovakia). Bratislava: SPACE, IVO, KcpRO.
